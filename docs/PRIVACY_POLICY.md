## Summary
Genesyx ("the app") is a fertility-preparation and cycle-tracking companion. **In this version,
Genesyx does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.** Everything you enter — your
cycle dates, daily logs, pH readings, notes, and preferences — is stored **only on your device**.

## Information we collect
**None.** The app has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking
SDKs. We do not have servers that receive your data, and we cannot see anything you enter.

## How your information is stored
All data you enter is saved locally on your device using the operating system's standard storage.
If you delete the app, that data is removed with it. You can also clear your data from within the
app (e.g. by editing or deleting entries).

## Health-related information
Genesyx lets you record health-related information (such as cycle dates, symptoms, hydration, and
urine-pH readings) for your own personal tracking. This information stays on your device and is
never sent to us or anyone else. Genesyx provides general educational and wellness content only
and is **not a medical device** and does **not** provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

## Sharing
Because we do not collect your data, we do not sell, rent, or share it with anyone.

## Children
Genesyx is intended for adults and is not directed to children.

## Your choices
- Edit or delete any entry within the app at any time.
- Delete the app to remove all locally stored data.

## Changes to this policy
If a future version of Genesyx adds optional accounts or cloud sync, this policy will be updated
to describe exactly what is collected, why, and how it is protected — and you will be informed
in-app before any data leaves your device.
